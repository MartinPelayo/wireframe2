Chocolate Pizza

Posted on 15 December 2013/Desserts

For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, util you have a chucky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granuled sugar until sugar is completley dissolved and the milk is just barley lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine. Pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig, jam mixture, and the mutes. continue to process for +/- minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tuppaware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cup milk

  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1 lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp H20
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream

  • 4/3 gradnuled sugar
  • 2 eggs yolks
  • 1 lemon juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans roughly chopped
